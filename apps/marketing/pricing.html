Questions, answered

How long does setup take?

Most restaurants are live within a week. Connect your POS, Google, and Instagram with guided setup — or send secure connect links to whoever holds the logins. The agents start working the first night.

Will the AI post things without my approval?

Only if you tell it to. Everything starts in draft — you approve reviews replies and posts until you trust the voice, then loosen the leash at your own pace.

What systems do you work with?

Square and Toast for POS, Google Business Profile, Instagram and Facebook, Dropbox for your media, and payroll via simple exports. More integrations land regularly.

Do I need to be technical?

No. If you can use email and Instagram, you can run Table Intelligence. Sage answers questions in plain language; the Monday Brief reads like a note from a sharp operations manager.

What happens to my data?

It stays yours. We use it to run your agents and produce your insights — never to train public models, never sold. Details in our privacy policy.
